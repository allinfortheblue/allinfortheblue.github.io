About the Event

Play for those who protect us

All In For The Blue brings the community together for a night of Texas Hold'em poker in honor of law enforcement. The winners of the tournament earn the right to choose the organizations — those that directly serve officers and their families — that receive all funds raised throughout the evening.

Please note: No player will win prize money. Tournament placement determines the right to select which organizations serving law enforcement officers and their families receive the funds raised that evening.
